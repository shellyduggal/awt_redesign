Go For Launch! returns to St. Edward's University July 10-12

Go For Launch! is a 3-day program that uses Space Exploration as a platform to launch student involvement in Science, Technology, Engineering and Math (STEM), STEAM (Art), teamwork, communication and leadership.

Students work side-by-side with Astronauts and other experts, competing for the chance to have their experiment launched into space.

The Go For Launch! experience gives students experience and skills that can lead to success in any venture that requires imagination, leadership, teamwork, and problem solving skills.
